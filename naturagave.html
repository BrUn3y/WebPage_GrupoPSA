The agave fields of Naturagave are the main suppliers of the Grupo PSA production plant. Each blue agave is nourished, harvested and planted in the best soil, climate and altitude for its optimum development.

Naturagave’s fields are strategically located in Jalisco, Michoacan and Nayarit. The main objective is to provide the best raw material to ensure products with international standards.
