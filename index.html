ABOUT US

We are a Mexican company, with global vision. We have presence in New Jersey, California, Netherlands and Spain. Our corporate offices are located in Zapopan, Jalisco.


All our products are supported by the four quality assurances; social and environmental, quality and food safety, organic and additional benefits that are reflected in the certifications that we have obtained over the years.


At Grupo PSA we have been nurturing agave for over 5 generations. This has been possible thanks to the commitment we have with our people. The development of our community, equal opportunities and the constant improvement of working conditions have been a philosophy in our organization since the beginning.


We are 100% vertically integrated, from our own blue agave fields to the production and distribution of the highest quality agave products. The core of our business, our production facilities, are located in Jiquilpan, Michoac√°n.
